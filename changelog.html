Transparent Encrypted Messaging

Intro

Written as a Encrypted Text Messaging application that can be reviewed easily and work no matter what. Website goes down? No problem. Internet gone, and have a copy downloaded? No problem.

How to Help

  • Help with translations.
  • Postcard graphics to leave lying around.

ChangeLog
